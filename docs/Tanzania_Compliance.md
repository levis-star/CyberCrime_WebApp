# Tanzania Compliance Notes

This project is an academic prototype and should be reviewed by qualified legal and law enforcement stakeholders before production use.

## Alignment Areas

- Tanzania Cybercrimes Act: supports structured incident intake, tracking, and evidence preservation.
- Personal data protection: anonymous submission, minimal identity collection, role-based access, and privacy notices.
- Evidence retention: uploaded files should have a documented retention schedule and access logs.
- Abuse prevention: rate limiting, validation, audit trails, and admin moderation reduce misuse.
- Responsible disclosure: reports should be routed to trained personnel before public alerts are issued.

## Operational Recommendations

- Publish clear consent text before evidence upload.
- Record who accessed or changed case data.
- Keep public alerts factual and avoid exposing victim identity.
- Provide escalation workflows for urgent threats, child safety, financial fraud, and harassment.
